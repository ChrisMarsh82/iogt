Developed regions have mostly 4G and 5G cellular service while developing regions have mostly 2G, 3G service. This gap in service means people in developing regions are unable to have a similar user experience to those in developed regions. Additionally, as modern websites take advantage of all the latest web technologies (which require fast internet/cellular speeds), users with lower speed packages will be unable to use these websites.
